4 Fruits to Eat for Dinner That Help You Lose Weight and Help You Sleep Better

There are so many delicious fruits out there, but it may be difficult to know which ones are best for your current health goals. Some fruits are high in fiber, and they may, therefore, be more effective at encouraging bowel movements than others.

You also get fruits that contain certain nutrients that may encourage sleep. There are also several fruits that can aid weight loss due to the different constituents found within them. If you'd like to learn more about the different fruits to eat that can aid weight loss and help you get a better night's rest, then read on.

Kiwi

Kiwis are a double whammy fruit when it comes to weight loss and quality sleep. These fruits are low in calories while being high in many essential nutrients that can benefit your gut health, reduce cholesterol, and fight inflammation. Kiwis render these benefits as they are loaded with carotenoid antioxidants as well as high levels of fiber. In terms of sleep, this fruit contains the sleep-promoting chemical serotonin, which can help to regulate a healthy sleep cycle. Kiwis also contain elevated levels of vitamin C, which has several anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects and can aid one in getting a good night's rest.

Tart Cherry

Tart cherries are not only delicious, but they also contain several compounds that can aid good sleep. Furthermore, they are also low in calories, which is great for those who are watching their weight. These morsels of goodness contain high levels of antioxidants, as well as modest amounts of nutrients potassium and magnesium potassium. Besides that, tart cherries also have high levels of melatonin, which is known for promoting sleepiness. For these reasons, you really can't go wrong with consuming tart cherries at night if you'd like to lose weight and get some good sleep.

Figs

Figs are great to consume for anyone who is on the path of losing weight while getting quality sleep. Figs are low-calorie fruits, and they contain high doses of fiber, which is great for the gut and weight loss. They are a sure source of essnetial nutrients such as with calcium, iron, magnesium, and potassium, which can also have a positive effect on one's sleep. These minerals encourage blood flow, and they can make falling asleep easier as they keep your muscles relaxed and free from contractions.

Pineapples

Pineapples were once a tropical delicacy reserved for the upper classes, but nowadays, you can find them in almost any supermarket and at any time of the year. This is great news because this fruit is great for those who want to lose weight while simultaneously getting a good night's rest.

Pineapples contain melatonin, and they can, therefore, boost one's chances of a good night's rest when consumed before bed. They are high in fiber and the bromelain enzyme, which is great for the digestive tract as it reduces inflammation and assists healthy bowel movements. This can have a great impact on one's weight management missions.
