5 Powerful Plus Size Fitness Influencers Who Motivate And Inspire

Curvy besties, the fitness world can feel a bit exclusive. We’ve all seen those perfect abs and tiny waists on our Instagram feeds. But guess what? Fitness comes in all shapes and sizes, and these five plus size fitness influencers are here to prove it. They’re breaking down barriers, showing you can be fit, fierce, and fabulous at any size.

Kicking Butt And Breaking Down Stereotypes

Plus size fitness influencers aren’t just about showing off their moves—they’re challenging the whole concept of what a “fit” body should look like. These influencers are all about body positivity, self-love, and smashing those old-school stereotypes, including diet culture. They make it clear that fitness is for everyone. Sharing their journeys, struggles, and victories inspires us to get moving and feel good in our own skin. They’re living proof that you don’t need to fit into a specific mold to be active and strong.

1. Jessie Diaz-Herrera (@curveswithmoves)

Jessie is a plus size certified fitness trainer and dance lover. Her high-energy routines are all about fun and self-expression. She makes moving your body feel like a celebration, not a chore. Her joyful vibe and killer moves will have you dancing in your living room. Jessie’s message is clear: fitness should be fun and accessible for all.

2. Isela Sanchez (@fitwithisela)

Isela’s feed blends workout tips, personal growth, and body positivity. She’s passionate about helping others find their inner strength. Her workouts are tough but doable, and her positive attitude is infectious. Isela shows that fitness is about progress, not perfection. She’s all about pushing yourself and finding joy in the journey.

3. Mirna Valerio (@themirnavator)

Mirna, also known as “The Mirnavator,” is a force to be reckoned with. She’s an ultramarathon runner, speaker, and author. Mirna’s story is all about perseverance and challenging the status quo. She’s out there running crazy long distances, proving that plus size bodies can achieve incredible feats. Her dedication and spirit are truly inspiring.

4. Chasity Jernigan (@sweatinmascara)

Chasity is a certified fitness instructor who combines fitness with glam, and we are here for it! Chasity shows that you can break a sweat and look amazing doing it. Her bold personality, fashion sense, and love for fitness make for a good time. Chasity proves that fitness and beauty go hand in hand.

5. Lizzy Howell (@lizzy.dances)

Lizzy is a dancer and advocate for body positivity. Her grace and strength shine through in every dance move. She’s breaking down barriers in the dance world and showing that talent comes in all sizes. Lizzy’s confidence and dedication to her craft are genuinely inspiring. She reminds us that our bodies are capable of amazing things.

These plus size fitness influencers are changing the game. They motivate us to embrace our bodies and find joy in movement. Whether through dance, running, or intense workouts, they show that fitness is for everyone. Follow them for daily doses of inspiration and to see what true body positivity looks like in action. So, get up, get moving, and join the revolution!
